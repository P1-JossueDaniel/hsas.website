Jossue Daniel is a novelist and a lover of rural life, blending his passions for literature and sports to craft stories that resonate deeply with readers. Writing has always been a channel for his creativity, offering both joy and a meaningful escape. His debut novel, Henry: Scars and Smiles, was completed with the loyal companionship of his beloved pet lovebird, Maggie, an Agapornis roseicollis, who was by his side throughout the writing process. Jossue brings a diverse skill set to his work, including experience as a Program Manager, legal expertise, and web development skills. Outside of his professional life, he volunteers his time to local sports committees, contributing to the community and encouraging youth involvement in sports. Currently, Jossue is working on his second novel, the title of which remains under wraps. In this upcoming work, the protagonist, Charlotte, creates a beautiful and intricate imaginary world called Ether.
