Hi, I'm Henry

I want to share my journey with you. Growing up in a quiet, nameless town in the 2000s, my life unfolds from childhood to young adulthood—ages 6 to 18. You'll witness the rollercoaster ride of my family's experiences, from joyful moments to tough challenges that test our resilience. As I navigate through life, I confront personal trials that shape my character: defending my family's honor, grappling with guilt from my own mistakes. Along the way, I meet Lilly, the love of my life. Together, we must navigate the complexities of our relationship, balancing her ambitious goals with our shared dreams. Ultimately, my story is about choices—deciding whether to persevere through adversity or to retreat, and discovering valuable lessons about love, loss, and the profound journey of personal growth. I invite you to join me on this heartfelt exploration of life's highs and lows.
